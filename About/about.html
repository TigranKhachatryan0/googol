About

Googol, (QuackQuackGo, Bang, BeginPage and OurTube -- coming soon) are ideas made back in January 2020. With these, you can search the web with a better design. The idea came into my mind when I first flashed LineageOS. If you didn't know, LineageOS is a very popular custom ROM at least recongnized by many Android users. I flashed LineageOS into my phone and was amazed by how amazing it is. It uses the material design that Google still uses in Android, but when they launched the Pixel, the material design in Android has became worse. But LineageOS still looks the good way. I discussed the ideas in an RCS chat and Stu, a member of the chat, gave me an idea on how to name it! (TheGrooglses). I'm thinking about going back to the old name, but TheGrooglses is not searchable online
